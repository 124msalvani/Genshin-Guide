Venti Guide

Venti is a Anemo Bow Character that specializes as a Support.

Overview

Venti is popular because of her high-risk, high-reward playstyle. The lower HP she is, the more damage you do, so a certain level of skill is required to play this character effectively. But those who do are rewarded with one of the best characters in the game.

Elemental Skill: Skyward Sonnet

Venti creates a Wind Domain, dealing AOE Anemo Damage. Press: Summons a Wind Domain at the opponent's location, dealing AoE Anemo DMG and launching opponents into the air.

Hold: Summons an even larger Wind Domain with Venti as the epicenter, dealing AoE Anemo DMG and launching affected opponents into the air. After unleashing the Hold version of this ability, Venti rides the wind into the air.

In combat, the Hold version should only be used to take advantage of specific mechanics, such as exposing the Ruin Hunter’s weak point. The animation takes too long, and Venti’s plunge damage is too low to justify otherwise.


Elemental Burst: Wind's Grand Ode

Venti fires off a Stormeye at a fixed distance away from himself. It will suck in enemies in a large radius. If the Stormeye comes into contact with Pyro/Hydro/Electro/Cryo, it will absorb that Element and deal additional Elemental DMG of that type.

The best crowd controller in the game. Be careful though, some characters can't hit enemies when they are lifted up by his ult.

Constellations

C1: Splitting Gales

Terrible constellation, completely useless unless you're going for a meme build.

C2: Breeze of Reminiscence

Decent damage increase, but nothing too impressive.

C3: Ode to Thousand Winds

+3 to his Burst. Pretty good for some extra damage.

C4: Hurricane of Freedom

Pretty useless unless you use Sacrifical Bow on him.

C5: Concerto dal Cielo

+3 to his Skill. Good for extra damage.

C6: Storm of Defiance

Not a bad constellation, but a terrible C6. There isn't a point to C6-ing him, unless you're a crazy whale.


Teams

Venti is a great Support and can fit on tons of teams. He's the best with Swirl Teams, but he can fit almost anywhere.
